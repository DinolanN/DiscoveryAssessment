Driving Profile
300 / 750
Each day you will be allocated 25 Drive points. Maintain your daily Drive points balance by driving well during the day by accelerating, braking and cornering smoothly, avoiding speeding and avoiding cellphone use.
will reduce your daily 25 Drive points balance, depending on the type and severity of the driving event.
At the end of every day, your remaining daily Drive points will bank towards your monthly Driving Profile, which is a build-up of your daily Drive points, up to 750 Vitality Drive points a month. This will contribute towards your monthly Vitality Drive points to earn up to 50% fuel cash back each month.

home safely.
